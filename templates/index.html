❓ Setup Guide — First Time Only
  1. Go to console.cloud.google.com → create a project (e.g. "PFC Routing").
  2. Go to APIs & Services → Library. Enable Google Sheets API and Google Drive API.
  3. Go to APIs & Services → Credentials → Create Credentials → OAuth 2.0 Client ID.
  4. Choose Web Application. Under Authorized redirect URIs add exactly: http://localhost:5050/api/google/callback
  5. Click Download JSON. Rename it to google_credentials.json and place it next to server.py.
  6. Go to OAuth consent screen → add your work email as a Test user.
  7. Restart the app, then click Connect Google Account above and sign in with your work account.
  8. Paste your Sheet ID above and click Sync!
